Here are some prominent government websites in India:

1. India.gov.in: The National Portal of India, serving as the official website of the Government of India.

2. MyGov.in: A citizen engagement platform for participatory governance, where citizens can participate in policy formulation and governance.

3. DigitalIndia.gov.in: This website is dedicated to the Digital India initiative, aimed at transforming India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy.

4. IncomeTaxIndia.gov.in: The official website of the Income Tax Department of India, providing information and services related to income tax filing and compliance.

5. ElectionCommissionIndia.nic.in: The official website of the Election Commission of India, responsible for administering elections in the country.

6. PMIndia.gov.in: The official website of the Prime Minister's Office (PMO) of India, providing updates on the Prime Minister's activities and initiatives.

7. RBI.org.in: The website of the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), the country's central banking institution, providing information on monetary policy, banking regulations, and financial stability.

These websites cover a wide range of government functions and services in India.
